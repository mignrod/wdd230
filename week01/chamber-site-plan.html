City of Barquisimeto, Lara state, Venezuela.

Barquisimeto Chamber of Commerce

Purpose

The Barquisimeto Chamber of Commerce strives to encourage business development in our city. Our higher purpose is offer our members a prosperous and equitable environment, where more and better businessmen, entrepreneurs, and companies can grow.

Scenarios

Events

The Chamber of Commerce will hold monthly events to ensure the participation and adaptation of all its members, promoting networking, training, and specialization in different commercial areas.

Board of Members

All members have access to information about Chamber's directors and members registred in the organization. Speechs and events will be promoted by principals according members needs.

Networking

The principal goal of the Chamber. Get to know your business and find commercial alliances that increase the productivity of your business, marketing strategies, and commercial proposals.
